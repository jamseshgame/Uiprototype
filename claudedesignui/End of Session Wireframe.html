Two reward states: just unlocked (lavender + green chip + "NEW" sticker) vs still locked but progressed (dashed, muted, with a progress bar showing how close they got). Always show both — pull next session forward
Currency reveal shows the math. Base + bonuses listed inline so the number feels earned, not arbitrary. Transparency = trust
Level-up moment is its own card with a tiny progress bar toward the next level — never bury a level-up inside the coin chip. Hero moment
Rewards are identical for host and invitees — same coins, same XP, same unlock eligibility. No "Bandleader" tax or bonus. Hosting is a convenience role, not a reward tier. Resolved · ship it
Animation: cards drop in one-by-one with a slight bounce, unlocked first then still-locked — feels like a tally roll, not a wall of text. Pacing

Three primary actions, ranked left to right: Share (most novel + viral), Replay (lowest-friction "again"), Play new (full reset). Strongest action gets the accent
Auto-generated clip snips the user's peak streak window — they can play it inline, then pick a platform. Sharing is one extra tap, not a full editor. Frictionless
Replay = same setup. Same track, instrument, difficulty, same band roster if applicable. No re-config. "Run it back" energy
Play new track / band drops you back at /perform with the relevant tab pre-selected (Solo or Multiplayer). For bands, the studio stays alive — only the track/Perform overlay closes. Continuity
in band mode, who controls "Replay"? host only, or any vote-to-replay? Open Q
Editing the clip (trim, add caption, choose a different highlight) is a secondary path — opens a sheet from the Share button, not exposed by default. Power-user surface

"Share Clip" doesn't open a Jamsesh share sheet. It hands the .mp4 off to Quest device storage, fires a system "saved" toast, then bows out. We're a guest in Meta's OS
Confirmation toast inside Jamsesh tells the user where the clip went and points to the Meta button on the right controller — the real share path lives in Quest UI. Discoverability
File naming = Track_Artist_Highlight_YYYYMMDD.mp4, so the file is recognizable once it's mixed in with other Quest captures. Convention
Most users won't share immediately. The clip just needs to be there next time they pick up the headset or open the Meta phone app — that's where the actual posting happens. Async share
Open Q: when a band shares, does each member get their own clip saved to their headset, or does only the host's headset get the file? Multiplayer share behaviour
