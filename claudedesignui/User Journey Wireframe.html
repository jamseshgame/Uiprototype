PLAY ▶ (3/4)
Auto-spawn, no venue picker. Once invitees accept, the whole group materializes inside the host's Personal Studio (or the band's Default Band Space if invited via a band). No lobby in between. One predictable destination
3D room is the stage — full-screen render, avatars in the space. The loadout picker is a translucent HUD overlay anchored top-right, not a side panel. Room reads first; config sits on top of it. Room-first, UI-overlay
Instrument + difficulty are picked here — each player chooses for themselves, visible to everyone in the roster. Personal choice, public state. Per-player loadout, in-room
Voice + chat are live in the space. Players hang out, mess around in the room while configuring — no rush to start. Pre-game social time
START is gated on readiness. The host fires once every player has locked in instrument + difficulty — then the session begins from inside the studio. Ready check terminates the flow
